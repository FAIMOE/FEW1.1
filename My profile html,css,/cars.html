Lamborghini Model

Lamborghini Revuelto x Mansory

The Lamborghini Revuelto Mansory "Initiate" boasts a combined power output of 1,070 horsepower from its V12 engine and three electric motors.

Model interview
Exterior Rim Interior Performance Speed
With the "MANSORY Initiate" based on the Lamborghini sports car Revuelto, MANSORY offers a complete customization programme for the Revuelto and at the same time its first refined hybrid sports car from Lamborghini ever. New rim design type "FV.10" in 21 & 22 inches (forged) The entire interior of the "MANSORY Initiate" can also be completely refined in all areas - depending on the customer's wishes - so that MANSORY customers can also achieve the highest possible degree of customization in the interior of their vehicle. The visual changes to the "MANSORY Initiate" go hand in hand with various modifications to the drive train and include subtle but nevertheless very effective changes to increase performance. enabling it to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 2.4 seconds and reach a top speed of 354 km/h (220 mph)

Price from RM4.1 million - RM7 million
